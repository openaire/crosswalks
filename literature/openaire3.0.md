# Crosswalk to [OpenAIRE Guidelines for Literature Repositories 3.0](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.1487968)

## Introduction

The section covers existing Crosswalks to the "[OpenAIRE Guidelines for Literature 3.0]()" from
* [RIOXX 2.0](https://www.rioxx.net/profiles/v2-0-final/)<br/>This crosswalk has been developed by the RIOXX team at EDINA in close collaboration with technical personnel at OpenAIRE. We would like to thank, in particular, Jochen Schirrwagen and Paolo Manghi for their assistance with this.<br/>
  For guidance on the exact representation of properties within the OpenAIRE 3.0 metadata profile, please consult the documentation [OpenAIRE Guidelines for literature, institutional, and thematic Repositories](https://guidelines.openaire.eu/en/latest/literature/index.html)
  The terms **MUST**, **MUST NOT**, **REQUIRED**, **SHALL**, **SHALL NOT**, **SHOULD**, **SHOULD NOT**, **RECOMMENDED**, **MAY**, and **OPTIONAL** used in the table below should be interpreted as described in [RFC 2119](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc2119).<br/>
  (Source: https://www.rioxx.net/mappings/crosswalk_rioxx_2_0_openaire_3_0/)

| dc:title             | *One or more*    | dc:title                    | *Exactly one*      | This **MUST** be included. | 
| dc:creator           | *One or more*    | rioxxterms:author           | *One or more*      | This **MUST** be included. The RIOXX property (in ISO8601 ‘YYYY-MM-DD’ format) MUST be translated into the appropriate OpenAIRE URI (info:eu-repo/semantics/dateAccepted/YYYY-MM-DD).<p>Example: RIOXX property:<br/><pre><dcterms:dateAccepted>  2015-03-16</dcterms:dateAccepted></pre></p>becomes OpenAIRE property:<br/><pre><dc:date>  info:eu-repo/semantics/dateAccepted/2015-03-16</dc:date></pre> |

| dc:date              | One or more      | rioxxterms:publication_date | *Zero or one*      | This **MUST** be included, if available and in a compatible format.
